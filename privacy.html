Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

ShootingCut LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ShootingCut application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

ShootingCut is designed with privacy in mind. The app processes video and audio data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal video or audio files on any external server.

We may collect the following limited information:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility purposes.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to help us improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and resolve technical issues
  • Understand general usage patterns to guide future development

3. Data Storage and Processing

All video analysis, audio processing, and editing operations are performed locally on your device. Your media files never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share them.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting (such as Apple's built-in analytics). These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing companies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about any data we have collected
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection through your device settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@shootingcut.com.
