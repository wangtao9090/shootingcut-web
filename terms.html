4. App Features and Functionality

ShootingCut provides the following core features:

  • Audio Analysis: FFT-based detection of electronic timer beeps (2500–3500 Hz) and gunshot pulses in your imported videos.
  • Auto Trim: Automatic video trimming based on detected timer and last-shot timestamps.
  • Merge: Linear concatenation of multiple video clips into a single video.
  • Split Sync: Timer-synchronized dual-angle split-screen video composition.
  • Stage Mix: Multi-camera intelligent scene selection and automatic editing based on shooting/movement phases.
  • Subtitle Overlay: Real-time performance statistics (shot times, splits, total time) overlaid as subtitles on exported video.
  • Person / Subject Tracking: Optional on-device AI-powered tracking that keeps a selected subject centered in the frame during export. All processing is performed locally using Apple's Vision framework; no video data or tracking results are transmitted off your device.
  • iCloud Session Sync: Session names and lightweight metadata are optionally synced across your own Apple devices via iCloud Key-Value Storage. Video files are never synced through this mechanism.
  • Social Sharing: Optional upload of exported videos to TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook (requires separate authorization).
