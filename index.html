Our Product

ShootingCut™

The ultimate video analysis and editing tool built for competitive shooters. Analyze, review, and share your IPSC, USPSA, and IDPA match and training footage like never before.

ShootingCut Editor
🎯

Stage & Run Analysis

Break down each stage with shot-by-shot analysis. Track split times, transitions between positions, and identify areas for improvement in your match runs.

🤖

AI Shot Detection

Automatically detect shots fired, magazine changes, and movement transitions. AI-powered scene segmentation tailored for shooting sports footage.

⏱

Timer & Scoring Overlay

Overlay shot timers, hit factor calculations, and scoring data directly onto your video. Compare your performance across multiple runs side by side.

📊

Performance Tracking

Track your progress over time with detailed analytics. Monitor draw times, reload speeds, transition efficiency, and hit factor trends across matches.

🎬

Quick Edit & Export

Trim, annotate, and add slow-motion to key moments. One-click export optimized for YouTube, Instagram, and sharing with your shooting squad.

🤝

Community & Sharing

Share annotated match videos with your team or coach. Built for the IPSC, USPSA, and IDPA community — compare runs and learn from top competitors.
