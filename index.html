What is ShootingCut™?

ShootingCut™ is a smart video tool built for competitive shooters. Quickly align multi-angle footage, auto-edit your match videos, and analyze your shooting performance — all in one app.

No more hours of manual editing. Import your videos, let ShootingCut™ handle the rest, and share professional match clips in minutes. Available on macOS, iPad, and iPhone.
