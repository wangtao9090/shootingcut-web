Features

Everything You Need to Review Faster

From automatic shot detection to multi-camera editing, Shooting Cut™ handles the heavy lifting so you can focus on improving your technique.

🎵

Smart Audio Analysis

Automatically detects timer beeps and gunshot impulses from your video audio. Get precise split times, first-shot reaction time, and total stage time — instantly.

✂️

Auto Trim

Automatically cut out dead time before and after your stage. Set custom padding around the timer start and last shot, and export a perfectly trimmed clip every time.

📊

Performance Subtitles

Overlay real-time stats directly onto your exported video: shot numbers, split times, cumulative time, first-shot time, and best split. Six color options to match any background.

🔄

Split Sync

Place two camera angles side-by-side in a synchronized split-screen. The timer beep aligns both timelines automatically — perfect for POV + follow-cam comparisons.

🎬

Stage Mix

Feed 3+ camera angles and let Shooting Cut auto-edit a highlight reel. POV during shooting, follow-cam during movement — with intelligent cut timing at action boundaries.

📱

Native on All Platforms

A full-featured macOS app with sidebar layout, and a purpose-built iPhone/iPad experience with 6-tab workflow. One purchase, all your devices.

🔒

Privacy First — Your Data Stays Yours

All processing happens on-device. Videos sync via iCloud and Apple Photos — never uploaded to third-party servers. No account required, no sign-up, no tracking. Your match footage stays on your devices and in your personal iCloud.

🚀

One-Tap Multi-Platform Share

Export and share your edited clips to YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram — all from a single share sheet. No extra apps, no manual exporting. Go from raw footage to social post in minutes.

🎯

Portrait Tracking — Shooter Always Centered

Switch Export Ratio to 9:16 for TikTok & Reels, 1:1 for Instagram, or 16:9 for YouTube — then tap Track to select your target. The app follows the shooter with smart reframing, keeping them centered in every frame regardless of output ratio. One clip, every platform, perfectly framed.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shooting Cut free?

Shooting Cut is free to download with core features available at no cost. No account, no sign-up, and no tracking required. Premium features are available via a one-time purchase that covers all your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, and Mac).

How is Shooting Cut different from Shot Streamer or cyfrTimer?

Shot Streamer is web-based and requires uploading your videos to a server. cyfrTimer focuses on timing analysis and overlay export but doesn't offer video editing or trimming. Shooting Cut is a native app that processes everything locally on your device — with multi-camera sync (SplitSync & StageMix), automatic video trimming, AI Reframe for vertical export, and full offline support. No uploads, no waiting.

What cameras work with Shooting Cut?

Any camera that records video with audio — GoPro, DJI Action, Insta360, iPhone, or any other camera. As long as the timer beep and gunshots are audible in the video audio, Shooting Cut can detect and analyze them. For best results, ensure the microphone isn't blocked or muffled.

Is my video data safe?

100%. All video processing happens entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Videos sync only through your personal iCloud and Apple Photos. No third-party analytics, no tracking, no account required.
