Features

Everything You Need to Review Faster

From automatic shot detection to multi-camera editing, Shooting Cut™ handles the heavy lifting so you can focus on improving your technique.

🎵

Smart Audio Analysis

FFT-based signal processing automatically detects timer beeps (2500–3500 Hz) and gunshot impulses. Get precise split times, first-shot time, and total stage time — instantly.

✂️

Auto Trim

Automatically cut out dead time before and after your stage. Set custom padding around the timer start and last shot, and export a perfectly trimmed clip every time.

📊

Performance Subtitles

Overlay real-time stats directly onto your exported video: shot numbers, split times, cumulative time, first-shot time, and best split. Six color options to match any background.

🔄

Split Sync

Place two camera angles side-by-side in a synchronized split-screen. The timer beep aligns both timelines automatically — perfect for POV + follow-cam comparisons.

🎬

Stage Mix

Feed 3+ camera angles and let Shooting Cut auto-edit a highlight reel. POV during shooting, follow-cam during movement — with intelligent cut timing at action boundaries.

📱

Native on All Platforms

A full-featured macOS app with sidebar layout, and a purpose-built iPhone/iPad experience with 6-tab workflow. One purchase, all your devices.

Editing Modes

Four Modes for Every Situation

Whether you have one clip or a full multi-camera rig, Shooting Cut has the right mode for your workflow.

✂️

Auto Trim

1 video

Trim a single match video to the exact stage window, add subtitles and watermark, and export a clean clip ready to share.

Best for: single-angle recap clips
🔗

Merge

2+ videos

Stitch multiple clips into one continuous video. Drag to reorder, set per-clip scores, and control audio for each segment independently.

Best for: multi-stage match montages
⬛⬛

Split Sync

2 videos

Lock two angles in perfect sync using the timer beep. Choose from full-screen split or HUD layouts. Ideal for POV vs. follow-cam analysis.

Best for: dual-angle comparison
🎬

Stage Mix

3+ videos

Label each camera as POV, Follow, or Static. The app selects the best angle for each moment — POV during shooting, follow for movement — and lets you override any cut.

Best for: cinematic multi-cam edits
